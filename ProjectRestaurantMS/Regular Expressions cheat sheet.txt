1. ^ means start with
2. $ means End with
3. [] means range
4. () means group
5. .- means Single character once
6. + means one or more characters in a row
7. ? means optional preceding character match
8. \ means escape character
9. \n means new line
10. \d means digit
11. \D means non-digit
12. \s means white space
13. \S means non-white space
14. \w means alphanumeric/ underscore characters (word chars)
15. \W means non-character words
16. {x, y} means repeat low (x) to high(y) 
17. (x|y) alternative, x or y
18. [^x] means anything but x ( x can be whatever character we want)
